It is possible to write software that solves real business problems, cheaply and reliably. The recipe is well known, even though it's not easy to do.

I am an Extreme Programmer. I help individuals, teams and organization become more effective at working with software. I work as a Technical Principal for Thoughtworks Italia. I used to teach at Università dell'Insubria.
